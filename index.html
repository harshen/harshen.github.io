Stories

Stories

My Contribution:

  • Server side development - PHP
  • Javascript/jQuery
  • HTML/CSS
  • Drupal - Content Management
www.storiesapps.com

Overview:

Stories is a social media marketing website. With stories, company admin (company owner) can create marketer's, content creators for his company brand, who can make and publish posts (scripts) for their company brand on Facebook, Twitter, Linkedin, Tumblr. With every user activity (likes & comments) of published post on Facebook and other networks, the post publisher will earn reward points which can be redeemed into gift cards. Stories also has an iPhone app which can be used by brand followers. With its help, brand followers can personalize the scripts by marketer's, admins and publish it on facebook and other networks for their respective friends to perform activity. The web application also provides statistical analysis.

Year: 2013
